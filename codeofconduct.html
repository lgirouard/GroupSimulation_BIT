Codes of Conduct

Professionalism

All employees must act with professionalism at all times, treating colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and courtesy.

Safety

The safety of all individuals involved in rocket software development is of utmost importance. All employees must follow safety protocols and report any safety concerns to their manager immediately.

Confidentiality

All employees must respect the confidentiality of the company's proprietary information, as well as any confidential information shared by customers or partners.

Quality

All employees must strive to produce high-quality software that meets or exceeds industry standards and customer expectations.

Diversity and Inclusion

The company is committed to fostering a workplace that values diversity and inclusion. Discrimination, harassment, or any other form of inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.

Communication

Clear and open communication is essential to the success of the company. All employees are encouraged to communicate openly and honestly with their colleagues, managers, and customers.

Accountability

All employees must take responsibility for their actions and be accountable for their work. This includes being honest about mistakes and taking steps to rectify them.

Continuous Learning

The company values continuous learning and encourages employees to stay up-to-date with industry trends and advancements in rocket software development.

Teamwork

Successful rocket software development requires collaboration and teamwork. All employees are expected to work collaboratively with their colleagues and contribute to a positive team culture.

Compliance

All employees must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies. Violations of any of these policies may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
