StarDrop

Our proprietary code-base for space-cargo landing and unloading uses advanced algorithms to navigate and identify landing sites in real-time. It interfaces with other systems on board through APIs and libraries, and has undergone rigorous testing to ensure reliability and safety. This code is essential to the spacecraft's ability to deliver cargo autonomously.

ExoBioDetect

Our proprietary code-base for detecting alien life on planets is optimized for efficiency and reliability in space conditions. It uses algorithms and machine learning models to analyze data from sensors on board the spacecraft, such as spectrometers and cameras. The code operates autonomously and interfaces with other systems through APIs and libraries. We have implemented rigorous testing and quality control procedures to ensure the code's safety and reliability.
